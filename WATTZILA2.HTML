ABOUT WATTZILLA

WattZilla is an advanced, custom-built Single-Phase Power Quality Analyzer. In modern electrical grids, non-linear loads (like LED drivers and switching power supplies) cause severe harmonic distortions. WattZilla is designed to monitor, analyze, and report these power quality metrics in real-time. By integrating a dedicated AI assistant (WattZilla AI), the system not only measures data but also helps engineers interpret complex electrical phenomena, bridging the gap between raw data and actionable engineering insights.

TECH SPECS

  • » Core Architecture: Developed an ESP32-based high-speed data acquisition system with real-time signal processing and FFT analysis for accurate electrical measurements.
  • » Sensing Modules: High-precision voltage and current sensing network utilizing a ZMPT101B voltage transformer - a CT sensor for isolated current measurement and op-amp-based signal conditioning for accurate ADC interfacing.
  • » Signal Processing: Custom implementation of Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) algorithms in C++ for real-time frequency domain analysis.
  • » Key Measurements: Real-time electrical power analysis featuring True RMS voltage and current measurement - Total Harmonic Distortion (THD) calculation - Active & Reactive Power estimation - and Fundamental Frequency tracking.
  • » Smart Features: Integrated AI assistant (WattZilla AI) providing real-time engineering assistance - circuit analysis - and technical problem-solving.
